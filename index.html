BillMint – Delete Account & Data

This page explains how to delete your BillMint account and data.

Choose the option that applies to you: guest (no account) or signed in with Google. You can email us or submit the form below.

If you don’t use an account (guest mode)

Your data only lives on your device. To remove it:

  • Uninstall the app, or
  • Clear app data (Settings → Apps → BillMint → Storage → Clear data).

No server data exists for guest users.

If you signed in with Google

You can request deletion of your account and all cloud data we store.

How to request deletion

1) Email us

Send an email to contact@francisoyitoba.com from the same email you used to sign in.
Subject: BillMint – Delete my account

  • Account email: (the Google email you used in BillMint)
  • Business name: (as set in your profile)

2) Fill the form on this page

Provide the same details so we can verify ownership.

Use the same email you used to sign in to BillMint.

What we delete

  • Documents (receipts & invoices)
  • Customers
  • Business profile (name, address, phone, payment info, currency, logo URL)

Data on your device isn’t deleted by us—please uninstall or clear app data if needed.

What we may retain (temporarily)

For fraud prevention, dispute handling, and legal compliance we may keep:

  • Minimal server logs and backups for up to 30 days, then permanently purged.
  • Aggregated, de-identified analytics that cannot identify you.

Timeline & confirmation

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 7 days. You’ll receive an email confirmation when deletion is done.

Can I cancel the request?

Yes—reply to our confirmation email before deletion is completed.

Need help?

Email contact@francisoyitoba.com and we’ll assist you.
